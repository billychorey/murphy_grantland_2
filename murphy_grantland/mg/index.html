About Us

In April 1997, J. R. Murphy and John M. Grantland founded the firm along with the help of a paralegal and receptionist. With the mission of providing high-quality legal services with uncompromising integrity and uncommon value, Murphy & Grantland flourished and more than doubled its client base and workload. In the next decade Murphy & Grantland expanded to include over a dozen attorneys, paralegals, legal assistants, file clerks, as well as a human resource area, bookkeeping area, and information technology area. After outgrowing three different office locations, Murphy & Grantland built its permanent location at 4406-B Forest Drive in Columbia.

We specialize in specific areas of representation that include Insurance Coverage and Bad Faith, Premises Liability, Insurance Defense, Trucking Defense, Products Liability, Construction Defects, Employment Law, Medical and Professional Malpractice Defense and Workers' Compensation.
